Training Buggle

Today, your buggle wants to get some serious exercise: It wants to run 'till the track burns! Its super-shoes are just perfect to run like hell, but unfortunately, they can actually damage the track on the long run...

Your goal is to run the track 10 times, no matter what happens. Even if the track suffers, you really HAVE to take that run. Remember, the track has four sides, that take eight steps each to run along. Now go, and show them what these super shoes can do.

You don't have to do anything particular with regard of the colors: your shoes will do it for you. Just take that run!
