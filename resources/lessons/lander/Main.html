Lunar Lander

In this lesson you pilot a lunar landing module in increasingly complex situations.

At each step of the simulation, the player issues a command controlling how much the angle and thrust of the lander should change

Ideas concerning the progression of exercises:

  • A lander in vertical position above flat ground, the player can only control thrust (done)
  • A lander in vertical position not above flat ground, the player control thrust and angle (done)
  • A lander with difficult angle and speed not above flat ground, the player control thrust and angle
  • A lander with difficult angle and speed not above flat ground, with difficult ground, the player control thrust and angle
  • Same with wind?
