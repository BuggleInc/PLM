BuggleWorld

This world was invented by Lyn Turbak, at Wellesley College. It is full of Buggles, little animals understanding simple orders, and offers numerous possibilities of interaction with the world: taking or dropping objects, paint the ground, hit walls, etc.

Note on exceptions

Regular buggles throw a BuggleWallException exception if you ask them to traverse a wall. They throw a NoBaggleUnderBuggleException exception if you ask them to pickup a baggle from an empty cell, or a AlreadyHaveBaggleException exception if they already carry a baggle. Trying to drop a baggle on a cell already containing one throws an AlreadyHaveBaggleException exception.

SimpleBuggles (ie, the one used in first exercises) display an error message on problem so that you don't need to know what an exception is.
