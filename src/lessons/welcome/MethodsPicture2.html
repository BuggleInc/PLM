We will now reproduce an even bigger geometrical drawing. Once again, you can see the model by clicking on the "Objective" tab.

You can naturally reuse all the code you typed in previous exercise (select the other exercise, do Ctrl-C, come back to the code of this exercise, do Ctrl-V).

But you want to keep your run() as simple as possible. For that, define new methods to deal simply with the repetitions in the pattern. For example, a method makePattern() achieving the pattern of previous example seems to be a good idea (but this may not be enough).

Why don't you give it a shot? The run() method shouldn't take more than 2 lines (included in a for loop)
